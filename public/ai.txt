# Atherstone Digital

Atherstone Digital is a Shopify ecommerce consultancy for brands doing £500k to £5M in annual revenue, based in Warwickshire, UK. One senior operator, Dan Le Gresley, runs the whole Shopify ecommerce function for each client, for less than an agency.

## What it is
A senior Shopify ecommerce consultant who owns the strategy and does the work: writing, building, ads, analysis, and fixing. No juniors, no relay handoffs. Three partners at a time. Retainers from £3,000/month on a rolling monthly contract.

## Who it is for
Shopify and Shopify Plus brands doing £500k to £5M in annual revenue who want one accountable senior operator instead of an agency roster or a full-time hire.

## Services
- Shopify store builds
- Conversion rate optimisation
- Google and Meta ads
- Shopify automation
- Google Shopping feeds
- Email and retention (Klaviyo)

- Custom development
